My MCO Experience


Grocery Shopping


Hi! During the movement control order (MCO), I had an experience where I was my mom's representative for groceries at a supermarket near my home. At that time, people were not too numerous because only one family member was allowed to make the purchase. In addition, I must always be careful and follow the rules set by the goverment during the MCO. So, stay at home to break the COVID-19. CLICK HERE

There are several steps when buying groceries during the MCO:

  • Make a list before going to the store to buy
  • Make sure to wear a mask
  • Make sure wear sanitizer in your hand
  • Prioritize food items that can be stored for a long time
  • Do not make 'panic buying'
  • Obey the 1-meter social distance directions
